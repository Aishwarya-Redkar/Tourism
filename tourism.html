Goa is a state situated at the west coast of India.Goa has 11 talukas & 2 districts namely north district and south district.Goa is a land of beautiful seashores flora and fauna. The main language of Goa is Konkani.People of different religions & different

cultures earn & live happily in Goa. Forest Cover in Goa is diverse.Goa is India's smallest state terms of area & the fourth smallest in terms of population. Goa is endowed with over 1512 documented species of plants, over 275 genera of birds, over 48 genera of animals & over 60 genera of reptiles.
